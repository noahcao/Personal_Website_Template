I am a Robotics PhD student in CMU with Kris Kitani since 2020.

Prior to CMU, I received bachelor degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2020, advised by Cewu Lu. I visited UC Berkeley in 2019, advised by Fisher Yu and Trevor Darrell. I also visited Yang Gao's group at Tsinghua University during COVID in 2020. I have interned in Meta Reality Labs, Tencent and Bytedance AI Lab etc. My research is supported by Meta PhD Fellowship since 2023.

I am interested in the human motion and behavior modeling in 3D and physical world. Feel free to reach out for collaboration and discussion of research ideas.
